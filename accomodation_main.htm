Which hotel is good for stay?

The conference venue is near Aobayama station of subway Tozai Line. Thus we recommend you to stay a hotel near a station of Tozai Line. Sendai Station is a station of subway Tozai Line, Namboku Line and the JR line, so it is a good idea to stay a hotel around Sendai station.
