The Fourteenth International Conference on Intelligent Information Hiding and Multimedia Signal Processing (IIH-MSP 2018), is technically co-sponsored by Research Institute of Electrical Communication, Tohoku University in Japan, Fujian University of Technology in China, Taiwan Association for Web Intelligence Consortium, and IEEE Signal Processing Society Sendai Chapter.

The conference proceedings will be published in the book series of Smart Innovation, Systems and Technologies by Springer. Normally, papers in this series are indexed by EI.
