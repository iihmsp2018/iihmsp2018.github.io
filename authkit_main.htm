Author's Guide

Announcement

The conference proceedings will be published in the book series of Smart Innovation, Systems and Technologies by Springer. Normally, papers in this series are indexed by EI. If the paper is not presented in the conference, the paper will be excluded from the conference proceedings.

Preparing Papers

The papers should be prepared according to Springer's computer science proceedings guideline. You can also find the paper templates for LaTeX2e, Microsoft Word (newer format, docx) and Microsoft Word (older format, doc). The paper should be no less than six pages. Before preparing the paper, please take a look at the Guidelines for proceedings authors.
