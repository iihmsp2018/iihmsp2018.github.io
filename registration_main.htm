Conference Registration

All attendees for IIHMSP-2018 are kindly requested to register. Related forms and supporting documents must be received with payment in US$ (US Dollar) no later than August 15, 2018. Please note that at least one author per paper must register before August 15, 2018. Otherwise the paper will not be included in the IIHMSP-2018 technical program and proceedings. Please also note that the page length limit is 6-8 pages. Extra pages will incur extra page charges. Each paper is allowed to have maximum 2 extra pages with raising USD 100 registration fee per extra page.

The registration fee includes conference proceedings, published by Conference Publishing Services, technical sessions, welcome reception, coffee breaks, lunches, and a banquet. An official receipt for the payment will be offered to the registrant on the conference site.

Please send us the completed Registration Form (Page 2) and a scanned copy of the receipt for the transfer from your bank by E-mail to: jchuang@tw-ubiquitous.com and iihmsp2018@spcom.ecei.tohoku.ac.jp.
